Our Process

90-Day Direct-to-Patient Growth Sprint

A structured 3-month engagement to build the system and deliver measurable momentum.
Month 1
Strategy + Foundations Build the plan + the measurement
01 Market Intelligence
Goal: Remove uncertainty before launching or increasing budgets.
  • Measure real demand (search volume, trends) by service + intent + geography
  • Analyze competitors (positioning, offers, channels, messaging, funnel paths)
  • Forecast expected CPL/CPA/CAC ranges before scaling
Outcome: Market report + channel strategy + expected unit economics.
02 Funnel & Offer Strategy
Goal: Define who we target, what we offer, and the path to conversion.
  • Map segments (cold/warm/hot) + intent tiers
  • Build the funnel logic (first click → booked/paid/qualified outcome)
  • Define offers, CTAs, and messaging per stage
Outcome: Funnel map + offer/CTA framework + channel-to-funnel plan.
03 Website & Landing Page Build
Goal: Make sure traffic lands on something that converts — not just something that looks good.
  • Audit existing site/UX and identify conversion leaks
  • Build or rebuild landing pages for services/offers
  • Improve mobile experience, speed, trust elements, and compliance
Outcome: High-converting website/landing pages ready for scaling.
Month 2
Build Conversion Assets + Launch Turn strategy into results
04 Tracking Foundation
Goal: Get maximum clean data to make correct decisions.
  • Track key conversions: calls, forms, bookings, registrations, purchases
  • Connect platforms end-to-end so we know what truly drives revenue
  • Capture quality signals where possible (lead quality, stage progression, show rate)
Outcome: Full-funnel visibility + reliable attribution for optimization.
05 Enhanced Analytics & Dashboards
Goal: Give leadership and teams real-time visibility into what's working and what's not.
  • Build comprehensive dashboards: website performance, marketing ROI, retention metrics
  • Track sales pipeline, conversion rates, and revenue attribution by channel
  • Monitor LTV, CAC payback periods, and cohort-level retention trends
Outcome: Live dashboards covering performance, sales, LTV, and retention.
06 Campaign Launch/Rebuild
Goal: Build acquisition that can scale without collapsing.
  • Set up or rebuild Google/Meta structure around intent + funnel stages
  • Align ads + landing pages + offers (message match)
  • Implement waste controls (search terms, targeting, exclusions, budget discipline)
Outcome: Campaigns that generate consistent volume with controllable CAC/CPA.
Month 3
Scale + Automate Make it compound
07 Daily Optimization
Goal: Win small improvements daily — which becomes massive over a year.
  • Daily analysis of spend, results, auctions, creatives, audiences, and intent
  • Reduce waste and unlock efficiency (keywords, bids, budgets, structure)
  • Disciplined iterations with measurable impact
Outcome: Lower CPL/CPA/CAC, better volume, stable performance.
08 Conversion Nurture
Goal: Convert users who don't buy/book on the first visit.
  • Multi-step retargeting funnels by stage
  • Email/SMS follow-ups tied to behavior and funnel stage
  • Automation from first touch to conversion (plus no-show reduction)
Outcome: More conversions from the same traffic + shorter decision cycle.
09 Retention & Repeat
Goal: Increase lifetime value and reduce dependency on constant acquisition.
  • Retention and repeat flows in CRM/email
  • Segmentation and personalization
  • Post-conversion automations: upsells, reminders, referrals
Outcome: High-converting website/landing pages ready for scaling.
